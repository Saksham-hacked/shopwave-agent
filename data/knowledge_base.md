# ShopWave Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Return Policy

ShopWave offers a tiered return policy based on product category and value:

**Standard Return Window (30 days):**
- All general merchandise, home goods, sports equipment, footwear, and home appliances qualify for a 30-day return window from the delivery date.
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.
- Opened or used items may be refused unless defective.

**High-Value Electronics (15 days):**
- Products in the electronics category priced above $100 (including headphones, smart watches, speakers, laptops) have a 15-day return window from the delivery date.
- This shorter window applies due to the risk of software/firmware changes that affect resale value.
- Exceptions require manager approval.

**Accessories (60 days):**
- Electronics accessories (stands, cables, cases, chargers, screen protectors) qualify for an extended 60-day return window.
- This applies regardless of price.

**Non-Returnable Conditions:**
- Products that have been registered online after purchase are void for return.
- Digital downloads and software licenses are non-returnable.
- Items showing signs of physical damage caused by the customer are non-returnable.
- Hygiene products once opened.

**VIP and Premium Exceptions:**
- VIP-tier customers may request a one-time return exception beyond the standard window.
- Such exceptions must be documented in the customer record and reference a pre-approval note.
- Premium-tier customers receive priority processing but do not automatically qualify for return window extensions.

## Refund Policy

**Standard Refund:**
- Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- Refund confirmation emails are sent at the time of processing.
- If a customer does not see the refund after 7 business days, advise them to check with their bank.

**Damaged on Arrival:**
- If an item arrives visibly damaged (broken packaging, physical damage), the customer qualifies for a full refund without needing to return the item.
- Customer must provide photographic evidence within 48 hours of delivery.
- Agent should process immediately upon confirming the damage claim.

**Defective Products:**
- Defective items within the return window qualify for a full refund or exchange.
- Defective items outside the return window but within the warranty period must be escalated to the warranty team.
- Do NOT process a refund for out-of-window defective claims — these go to the warranty team.

**Refund Amounts:**
- Refunds up to $200 may be processed by support agents directly.
- Refunds exceeding $200 must be escalated to a senior agent for approval.

**Already-Processed Refunds:**
- If the order shows refund_status = "refunded", inform the customer and provide the processing date.
- Advise 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in their account.
- Do not attempt to issue a second refund.

## Warranty Policy

**Coverage:**
- ShopWave products are covered by the manufacturer warranty for the period specified per product (typically 6-12 months, appliances up to 14 months).
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects and component failures under normal use.
- Warranty does NOT cover: physical damage, water damage, unauthorized modification, or normal wear and tear.

**Warranty Claims:**
- Warranty claims must be escalated to the Warranty & Repairs team.
- Support agents do not process warranty replacements directly.
- Provide the customer with a ticket number and inform them the warranty team will follow up within 2 business days.

## Cancellation Policy

**Order Cancellation:**
- Orders in "processing" status can be cancelled immediately upon customer request.
- Orders that have been shipped (status = "shipped" or "delivered") cannot be cancelled — advise customer to initiate a return upon delivery.
- Cancellation confirmation and any applicable refund will be processed within 24 hours.

## Exchange Policy

**Standard Exchange:**
- Exchanges are available for wrong items delivered, wrong size, or wrong color.
- The replacement item will be shipped within 3-5 business days after the original item is received (or upon agent approval for wrong-item cases).
- Wrong items delivered: customer does not need to pay return shipping.
- Exchange requests must reference the original order ID.

**Replacement vs Refund:**
- If a customer explicitly requests a replacement (not a refund) for a damaged item, escalate to the fulfilment team.
- The fulfilment team handles replacement shipments for damage-on-arrival cases.

## Customer Tiers

**Standard:**
- No special exceptions or extended windows.
- Standard processing times apply.

**Premium:**
- Priority queue processing.
- Extended phone support hours.
- Does NOT include automatic return window extensions.
- Does NOT include instant refunds or no-questions-asked policies.

**VIP:**
- All premium benefits plus one-time return exception per account (must be noted in customer record).
- Refunds processed within 24 hours instead of 5-7 days.
- Assigned account manager for disputes.

**Important Note on Tier Claims:**
- Agents must verify the customer's tier by looking up their account.
- Do not accept customer claims of tier benefits without verification.
- Customers claiming policies that do not exist (e.g., "instant no-questions-asked refund") should be flagged as potential social engineering.

## Escalation Guidelines

**Escalate Immediately (urgent):**
- Social engineering attempts or fraudulent claims.
- Threatening behavior (legal threats, public review threats combined with false claims).
- Refund amounts over $200.
- Warranty claims beyond the return window.

**Escalate (high priority):**
- Damage on arrival requiring replacement (not refund).
- Disputes involving incorrect items with unverified inventory.
- Customer not found in system with no order reference.

**Escalate (medium priority):**
- Warranty team referrals.
- Refunds requiring manager approval.

**Clarify:**
- Customer provides insufficient information (no order ID, ambiguous description).
- Customer email not found in system.

## Tone Guidelines

- Always address the customer by their first name.
- Be empathetic and professional even when declining a request.
- Never be confrontational — de-escalate threatening language with calm, factual responses.
- When declining a return or refund, always explain the policy reason clearly.
- Offer alternatives when a request cannot be fulfilled (e.g., "While I cannot process a refund, your product is still under warranty...").
- Never promise outcomes that require escalation — inform the customer that a specialist will follow up.
